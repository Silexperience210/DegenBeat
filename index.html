Bitcoin trading terminal for ESP32 & ESP32-S3.
Flash your board directly from this page — no software needed.

FIRMWARE v2.2.0
🔌 Supported hardware — auto-detected at flash time:
• Sunton ESP32-2432S028R — ST7789 320×240, XPT2046 touch, RGB LED (ESP32)
• LCDWiki ESP32-S3 2.8inch — ILI9341 320×240, FT6336U touch, RGB LED (ESP32-S3)
The correct firmware is selected automatically based on the chip detected in your board.
Flash Firmware
1
Connect your board
Plug your board into your PC via USB. No drivers needed on Windows 10+ / macOS / Linux.
2
Click Install below
Your browser will ask to select a serial port — choose CP210x / CH340 for Sunton ESP32, or USB JTAG/serial for ESP32-S3.
3
Wait ~30 seconds
The flasher writes bootloader, partition table, and firmware. The board reboots automatically when done.
4
Connect to LNMarkets-Setup
A WiFi access point appears. Open your browser, enter your WiFi credentials and LN Markets API keys.
Your browser does not support WebUSB. Use Chrome or Edge. Access denied. Make sure you are on HTTPS and the page is served (not opened as a local file).
